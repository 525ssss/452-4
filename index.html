Zheng Shuang transferred property or was sentenced

After being banned for "surrogacy abandonment" and "tax evasion", a few days ago, the new enforcement ruling in the contract dispute between actor Zheng Shuang and Zhejiang Haining Dongkai Star Film and Television Investment Co., Ltd. was issued by the Shanghai Songjiang District People's Court. Xiaoxiang Morning News (WeChat: xxcbbaoliao) reporter saw from the ruling that the court believed that after exhausting property investigation measures, Zheng Shuang, the person subject to enforcement in the case, had no property available for enforcement, and agreed to terminate the case enforcement procedure after interviewing with the person applying for enforcement.

Xiaomi SU7 unsubscribe rate 40%

Fast Technology reported on April 2 that a data report released by a blogger showed that 72 hours after the Xiaomi SU7 was listed, the average store accumulated 1800-2000 units. Among them, the average daily number of new stores is 700-1000 batches, the order lock rate is 35-40%, and the unsubscription rate is 40%. According to statistics, nearly 50,000 users have completed the lock order in 59 Xiaomi SU7 stores across the country.
